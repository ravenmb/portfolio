about me.

When life gave me lemons, I decided to pursue my lifelong dream and paint them as a still life. I have found my soul in oil paint. But while pursuing my Associates degrees in Studio Art and Art History, I discovered the joy that is editing a line of color until it is precisely the shape you want it to be. I got hooked on Graphic Design.

As I now pursue a BA in Graphic Design at CSU East Bay, I continue to discover new joys: illustration, print-making, photography, and even web authoring.
